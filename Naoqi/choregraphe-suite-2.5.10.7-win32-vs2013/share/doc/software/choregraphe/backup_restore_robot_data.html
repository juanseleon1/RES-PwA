Backing up / restoring robot data¶

Easy backup via Choregraphe¶

Cannot be tested on a simulated robot.

Step Action
Make sure your robot is ready, check: 1/5 - Making the Aldebaran robot ready for backup.
In Choregraphe, choose Connection > Advanced > Back up / restore robot data.
Select the back up action and click on the Next button.
Follow the different steps. For further details, check 3/5 - Connecting to the Aldebaran robot.
Once the backup is finished, click on the Finish button to close the window.

Easy restore via Choregraphe¶

Cannot be tested on a simulated robot.

Step Action
Make sure your robot is ready, check: 1/5 - Making the Aldebaran robot ready for restoration.
In Choregraphe, choose Connection > Advanced > Back up / restore robot data.
Select the restore action and click on the Next button.
Follow the different steps. For further details, check 3/5 - Connecting to the Aldebaran robot.
Once the restore is finished, click on the Finish button to close the window.

See also Backup/Restore robot data troubleshooting.
