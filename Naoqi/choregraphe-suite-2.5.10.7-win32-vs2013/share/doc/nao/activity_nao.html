Launching an ActivityÂ¶

Prerequisite

Autonomous Life: ON - Basic Channel: ON - Installed Activities: Yes

To start an Activity:

Step Action

Make sure NAO is listening.

For further details, see: Talking with NAO.

Say:

  • “Start” + the Activity name, or
  • one of the Trigger sentences, if any.

NAO automatically starts the corresponding Activity.

Exiting an ActivityÂ¶

Prerequisite

Autonomous Life: ON - Basic Channel: ON - Installed Activities: Yes

To stop an Activity currently running:

Step Action

Lay your hand on NAO‘s head:

  • palm on the top camera, and
  • fingers on the head tactile sensor

Hold your hand there for 1 second.

Feedback:

  • a slight sound: “blop”

Quickly remove your hand to stop the activity.

Feedback:

  • “click” sound, and eye and status LEDs become green for a short moment
Result After you remove your hand, the current Activity is stopped.
